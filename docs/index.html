About SUGANDHA ENTERPRISES

Sugandha Enterprises (SE), a proprietorship firm was established in 1993 as contractor & engineering firm deals in the verticals of electrical, mechanical & civil projects and is known for its quality, commitment and consistency in performance. We are specialised in complete engineering services, procurement and electrical works using state of art & technology in erection, installation, commissioning, testing & maintenance of sub-station, laying of underground LT/HT/EHV Cables, Installation & Maintenance of LT/HT/EHV Overhead and underground cabling systems according to desired Electrical Services.

We operate not only in competitive environment but in also difficult circumstances and regions, offering integrated solutions for Clients’ complex and challenging capital projects. The quality of work been delivered so far gives Clients the confidence that we can deliver complex, logistically challenging projects anywhere in the country.
